# Research Question 3: Vote Share Story

## Business Question

Where did TVK's votes come from?

Compare party vote share statewide and by region in 2021 vs 2026.

Did TVK pull votes from DMK, AIADMK, both, or from previously non-voting populations?

---

# Objective

The objective of this analysis is to understand how voter preferences shifted between the 2021 and 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Elections.

Instead of focusing on seat counts, this analysis examines vote share changes to identify which parties lost support and whether those losses correspond to TVK's emergence as a major political force.

---

# Methodology

1. Calculate total votes received by each party in 2021.
2. Calculate total votes received by each party in 2026.
3. Compute statewide vote share percentages.
4. Compare vote share changes between elections.
5. Analyze regional vote patterns across:

   * Chennai Metro
   * North
   * Central
   * Kongu
   * Delta
   * South
6. Identify which parties experienced the largest vote share decline.

# Key Findings

## TVK Emerges as a Major Political Force

TVK entered the 2026 election and immediately secured:

* 17.2 million votes
* 34.92% statewide vote share

This made TVK the largest party by vote share in Tamil Nadu.

---

## DMK Experienced Significant Decline

DMK's vote share fell from:

* 37.70% in 2021
* 24.19% in 2026

Loss:

-13.51 percentage points

---

## AIADMK Also Lost Major Support

AIADMK's vote share declined from:

* 33.29% in 2021
* 21.21% in 2026

Loss:

-12.08 percentage points

---

## Smaller Parties Also Lost Ground

Several parties experienced notable declines:

* NTK: -2.58%
* PMK: -1.63%
* AMMK: -1.49%
* INC: -0.90%

This indicates that TVK's rise was not limited to one political camp.

---

# Regional Analysis

## Chennai Metro

TVK became the dominant party in Chennai Metro and overtook both DMK and AIADMK.

DMK suffered its largest urban decline in this region.

---

## Central Region

TVK emerged as the largest vote-getter.

Both DMK and AIADMK recorded significant vote losses.

---

## Kongu Region

Historically an AIADMK stronghold.

TVK successfully penetrated the region and became the leading party by votes.

---

## South Region

South Tamil Nadu generated the highest vote total for TVK.

AIADMK experienced its largest regional decline.

---

# Business Conclusion

The evidence suggests that TVK did not draw support from a single party.

Instead, TVK attracted voters from:

* DMK
* AIADMK
* NTK
* PMK
* AMMK
* Various smaller regional parties

The 2026 election represents a broad statewide voter realignment rather than a simple anti-incumbency swing against one party.

TVK's success was built on a cross-party coalition of voters across multiple regions of Tamil Nadu.

---

# Recommendation

Future campaign analysis should focus on:

1. Constituencies where TVK gained the highest vote share.
2. Regional voter migration patterns.
3. Demographic segments most responsive to TVK's campaign.
4. Whether TVK's support base remains stable in future elections.

These areas can provide deeper insight into the sustainability of TVK's electoral growth.
