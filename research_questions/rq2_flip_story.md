# Research Question 2: The Flip Story

## Business Question

In how many constituencies did the winning party change between 2021 and 2026? What pattern emerged from these constituency-level shifts?

---

## Approach

1. Identified winning party in each constituency for 2021.
2. Identified winning party in each constituency for 2026.
3. Compared winners constituency-by-constituency.
4. Classified constituencies as:
   - Retained
   - Flipped
5. Created a party transition matrix for Sankey analysis.

---

## Constituency Stability

| Status | Constituencies |
|----------|------:|
| Flipped | 162 |
| Retained | 71 |

### Insight

69.5% of constituencies changed their winning party between 2021 and 2026, indicating a major political realignment across Tamil Nadu.

---

## Major Party Transitions

| 2021 Winner | 2026 Winner | Constituencies |
|------------|------------|---------------:|
| DMK | TVK | 64 |
| AIADMK | TVK | 26 |
| DMK | AIADMK | 22 |
| AIADMK | DMK | 15 |
| INC | TVK | 11 |

---

## Key Findings

### TVK was the biggest beneficiary

TVK gained:

- 64 seats from DMK
- 26 seats from AIADMK
- 11 seats from INC

Total seats gained through constituency flips: 107

### DMK was the biggest loser

DMK lost:

- 64 seats to TVK
- 22 seats to AIADMK

Total seats lost: 92

### Most Common Transition

DMK → TVK (64 constituencies)

This was the largest political shift observed in the election.

---

## Conclusion

The 2026 Tamil Nadu election was characterized by large-scale constituency-level shifts. TVK emerged as the primary beneficiary of these changes, while DMK experienced the largest erosion of previously held constituencies.

The dominant pattern was the transfer of constituencies from DMK to TVK.
