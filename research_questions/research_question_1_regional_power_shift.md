# Research Question 1: Regional Power Shift Analysis (2021–2026)

## Business Question

How did seat distribution shift across Tamil Nadu's regions between 2021 and 2026?

---

## Objective

Identify:

* Regional political dominance
* Seat gains and losses
* Emerging political parties
* Changes in regional leadership

---

## Analytical Approach

### Step 1

Identify winners in each constituency.

### Step 2

Calculate party-wise seat distribution.

### Step 3

Calculate region-wise seat distribution.

### Step 4

Compare 2021 and 2026 regional outcomes.

### Step 5

Generate business insights and recommendations.

---

## SQL Techniques Used

* ORDER BY
* GROUP BY
* MAX()
* COUNT()
* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* JOINs

---

| Region        | Leading Party |

## 2026 Regional Leaders

| Region        | Leading Party |
| ------------- | ------------- |
| Chennai Metro | TVK           |
| Delta         | DMK           |
| South         | TVK           |

| North         | TVK / AIADMK  |

---

## Power Shift Matrix

| Region        | 2021   | 2026         |
| ------------- | ------ | ------------ |
| Chennai Metro | DMK    | TVK          |
| Delta         | DMK    | DMK          |
| South         | DMK    | TVK          |
| Central       | DMK    | AIADMK       |
| North         | DMK    | TVK / AIADMK |
| Kongu         | AIADMK | TVK          |

---

## Key Findings

### Finding 1

DMK dominated five out of six regions in 2021.

### Finding 2

TVK emerged as the largest party in 2026 with 107 seats.

### Finding 3

TVK captured Chennai Metro, South, and Kongu.

### Finding 4

DMK retained leadership only in the Delta region.

### Finding 5

AIADMK recovered in the Central region.

---

## Business Recommendation

Political analysts and media organizations should closely monitor:

* TVK's rapid expansion
* DMK's declining regional dominance
* AIADMK's regional recovery patterns

---

## Conclusion

The 2026 election significantly altered Tamil Nadu's political landscape. TVK emerged as a major force, replacing DMK as the dominant party across multiple regions.
